French Toast

Home

Description

Who doesn't love a good French toast? Well, there's no better French toast than ours. It's full of tasty spices, rich buttery goodness, and is super easy to make. Bon apetit!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of sandwich bread
  • 1 egg, scrambled
  • 200 ml of milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp butter for frying
  • Maple syrup for topping
  1. Mix the egg, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt together.
  2. Dredge the bread until it's nice and soaked.
  3. Add 1/2 tbsp of butter to the pan for every slice you're fitting in. Let it melt, then add the bread, turning the heat down to low-medium. Fry on each side until golden brown.
  4. Serve with maple syrup or whipped cream, or whatever other topping you like!
